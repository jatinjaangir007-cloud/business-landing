Our Heritage

Crafting memories since 1998

Lumière was founded by the Beaumont family with a singular vision: to build a sanctuary where hospitality transcends service and becomes art.


Over 25 years, we have welcomed heads of state, celebrated artists, and families seeking the extraordinary. Our commitment remains unchanged — to honour every guest with unwavering attention, timeless surroundings, and the warmth of home.

1998

Lumière opens its doors with 12 rooms in a 19th-century townhouse.

2004

Expansion to 48 rooms and launch of Le Jardin, our signature restaurant.

2011

Opening of the Lumière Spa — our flagship wellness destination.

2018

Awarded Best Boutique Hotel in the region for the third consecutive year.

2024

Launch of our sustainability programme — carbon neutral by 2028.
